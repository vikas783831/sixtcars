About SixtCars

SixtCars is a global leader in premium car rental services, with over 100 years of experience in the mobility industry. We pride ourselves on offering an exceptional selection of vehicles combined with world-class customer service.

Our mission is to provide clients with access to the finest automobiles, whether for business, leisure, or special occasions. With locations in all major cities worldwide and 24/7 support, we're committed to delivering an unparalleled rental experience.

Every vehicle in our fleet undergoes rigorous maintenance and detailing before each rental, ensuring you receive a car in perfect condition every time.
